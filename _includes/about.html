About

Kaiyang Zhao is an independent audio developer, composer, and music producer, who is currently studying at Beijing Foreign Studies University. Kaiyang has developed several Max for Live devices that have been downloaded hundreds of times on maxforlive.com. He has also designed other computational or interactive music installations using Max. Besides, he has released many original music pieces on the internet.

Skill and proficiency: proficiency in Ableton Live, Max & Max for Live, virtual synthesizers such as Serum, and mainstream audio plugins such as Fabfilter, Kontakt, etc.
